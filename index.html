Essential Items Memory Jogger

When everything happens suddenly, it’s hard to remember even the everyday items you once had. This tool provides a simple, ready-made list of common household essentials to help you start rebuilding your inventory.

No pressure and no long forms — just an easy way to add rooms and remind yourself of the basics. Your inventory specialist will appreciate the head start.

Take things one step at a time. We’re here to help you begin.

How to move forward

  1. Write down each room you remember. Don’t worry about getting it perfect.
  2. Use the jogger to place common essentials in those rooms.
  3. Save anything personal or unique for your specialist later.

Keep things simple

Short sessions are okay. Add a few rooms today and return later — your progress is saved on your browser automatically.
